Beyond the Classroom

Language is lived. Join our student clubs to learn vocabulary through action.

1

Cinema & Theater Club

Watch, analyze, and act out scenes from foreign cinema to master colloquial slang and emotional expression.

2

Regional Excursions

Weekend trips guided entirely in your target language to historical sites, museums, and local markets.

3

Language Exchange Cafés

Bi-weekly casual meetups with local native students who want to practice your language while you practice theirs.

Scholarships & Aid

We believe immersion should be accessible. The institute offers merit and need-based scholarships covering up to 80% of tuition and housing.

Step 1: Submit Application

Apply for your target program online. Check the "Financial Aid Request" box.

Step 2: Provide Documentation

Upload required financial forms or academic transcripts via the Student Portal.

Step 3: Receive Award Letter

Decisions are sent within 14 days of document submission.
